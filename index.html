InAmigos Foundation

Uniting Minds for Change

InAmigos Foundation is a Government-licensed, ISO-certified non-profit. We work across 28 states with 200+ volunteers, 6 active causes and 50,000+ beneficiaries. We feed the hungry, educate children, empower women, protect animals and nurture our environment. Because real change begins when minds unite with purpose.

About Us

InAmigos Foundation was founded on September 23, 2020 by Mr. Govind Shukla in Bilaspur, Chhattisgarh. Licensed by the Central Government and registered under Section 8 of the Companies Act, we are a fully transparent and accountable organization. Our mission is simple. We unite minds and create lasting change across India.
